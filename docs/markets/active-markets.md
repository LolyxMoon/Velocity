## How to Participate

1. **Connect Wallet**
   - Visit [velocitybets.com](https://velocitybets.com)
   - Click "Connect Wallet"
   - Choose MetaMask, Rabby, or WalletConnect

2. **Choose a Market**
   - Browse active markets
   - Review details and odds

3. **Place Your Bet**
   - Enter amount (0.001 - 0.1 BNB)
   - Click YES or NO
   - Confirm transaction

4. **Monitor Progress**
   - Watch live odds
   - Track your position
   - Wait for deadline

5. **Claim Winnings**
   - After market resolves
   - Click "Claim Winnings"
   - Receive BNB payout
